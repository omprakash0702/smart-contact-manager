Our Services

Smart Contact Manager provides a set of powerful services that help users store, manage, and access their contacts securely from anywhere.

Secure Contact Storage

Store all your contacts securely in the cloud with encrypted passwords and protected access using Spring Security.

Contact Management

Add, update, view, and delete contacts easily through a clean and user-friendly dashboard.

Search & Filtering

Quickly search contacts by name, email, or phone number to find exactly what you need.

User Authentication

Secure login system with email/password authentication and future support for Google and GitHub OAuth login.

Cloud Accessibility

Access your contacts anytime, anywhere from any device with an internet connection.

Future Enhancements

Planned features include email notifications, contact sharing, analytics, and advanced role-based access control.
